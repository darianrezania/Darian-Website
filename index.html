About Darian

My name is Darian, and I am a 5th year studying behavioral neuroscience with a minor in datascience. I enjoy learning about how different scientific questions can be explored through pure research as well as data analysis. In my 5 years at Northeastern I have completed 3 co-ops and 2 different internships all in the field of cancer research. These experiences have given me a lot of insight into how science works in the real world, and they have also helped me develop a wide range of skills that I hope to continue building in the future. I am especially interested in how biology and AI can work with one another to create new tools for treating diseases, testing medications, and more.

I also enjoy working on data analysis skills that support scientific work. I added a minor in datascience so I could learn more about programming, data analysis, and visualization. I have learned to use data science to effectively represent experimental data and show the story behind the numbers
